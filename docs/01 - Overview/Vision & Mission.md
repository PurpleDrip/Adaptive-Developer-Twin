# Vision & Mission

## Vision

> A world where engineering decisions — who builds what, who mentors whom, who gets the stretch role — are grounded in **evidence, not politics**.

## Mission

Build the most reliable, least-manipulable source of truth about engineering capability inside an organization, and put that truth in the hands of:

- Developers (to grow with intention)
- Managers (to allocate with confidence)
- Leaders (to hire and promote with evidence)

## Why now?

1. **Code generation is a productivity multiplier.** When everyone can ship more code, *skill differentiation* matters more than ever — and self-reported skill has never been less reliable.
2. **Remote-first orgs lose tacit signal.** The hallway conversation is gone. A digital twin re-creates that signal — without surveillance.
3. **Org graphs are the new resumes.** Influence inside a company is the strongest predictor of promotion potential. PageRank-style centrality finally has the data to be useful.

## What we will NEVER do

- Sell developer-level data to third parties.
- Use the platform for individual disciplinary action without the developer's awareness.
- Lock customers in via proprietary data formats — all skill data is exportable.

## The bet

If we can make a Twin that developers *want* to be measured by — because it shows growth and unlocks opportunities — we win. If we make one that feels like surveillance, we lose.
